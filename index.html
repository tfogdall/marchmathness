WHAT IS MARCH MATHNESS??

March Mathness is a single-elimination mathematics tournament/festival inspired by the NCAA basketball tournaments and is held in March and April of each year. The event's primary goal is to foster and encourage collaborative problem-solving with a focus on simply having fun for students of all mathematical backgrounds. To accomplish the goal of making the event accessible to all, exercises are largely drawn from an area of mathematics called discrete mathematics, which usually requires very little prior knowledge to participate. Long story short: Students without advanced algebraic training are encouraged to participate!
